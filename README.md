# TON Space Killer

🚀 **TON Space Killer** is a cyberpunk-themed vertical space scroller and decentralized gaming Telegram Mini App. Control your ship, collect energy crystals, navigate perilous asteroid fields, and upgrade your hardware in the Hacker Garage while managing your decentralized balances using TON Connect and Telegram Stars!

---

## 🌌 Core Features

### 1. Retro-Futuristic Flight Engine
- **Maneuver & Dodge**: Snappy tap zones for mobile screens, dragging, and keyboard layouts (`A`/`D` or arrow keys).
- **Speed Warp Progression**: Play through 50 escalating rounds of 1 minute each, with speeds compounding by `+25%` per round.
- **Grand Victory**: Clear all 50 rounds to claim the ultimate grid championship, along with **+5,000 Crystals** and **+50 GRAM** premium tokens.

### 2. Hacker Garage (Ship Upgrades)
Upgrade 10 different hardware modifications:
- **Reinforced Deflectors (Shields)**: Increases maximum shield capacity.
- **Quantum Attractor (Magnet)**: Automatically pulls nearby crystals.
- **Plasma Burners (Engine)**: Boosts ship maneuverability and crystal scoring.
- **Nano-Repair Droids**: Regenerates ship shield integrity over time.
- **Glitch Mining Protocol**: Grants chances to spawn rare high-value glitch crystals.
- **Overclock Core**: Amplifies baseline crystal spawn rates.
- **Shockwave Burst**: Destroys nearby asteroids via automatic EMP pulses.
- **Targeting Matrix**: Locks onto and displays guidance vectors to the closest crystal.
- **Nano-Shield Capacitor (Premium)**: Absorbs one fatal collision per round. (Costs **GRAM** `💎`)
- **Crystal Singularity (Premium)**: Increases final completion yields (+20% per level). (Costs **GRAM** `💎`)

### 3. Decentralized Balance & Payments Tab (👛)
- **TON Connect Integration**: Link your Telegram Wallet, Tonkeeper, or Tonhub directly inside the app.
- **Pay with TON**: Bypasses local crystal limits to purchase garage upgrades directly with TON cryptocurrency via standard transaction signature requests.
- **DeFi Exchange**: Swap gathered crystals for premium GRAM tokens (10,000 Crystals = 1 GRAM) or buy GRAM with TON directly.
- **Telegram Stars Depot**: Purchase in-game resources (crystals or GRAM) using Telegram Stars (`🌟`) backed by a cyberpunk-themed checkout invoice dialog.

### 4. Data Privacy & Compliance (🔒)
- **Local Storage Policy**: Fully compliant with Telegram Mini App privacy rules. All game parameters, scores, and wallet indicators are saved 100% locally on your device via `localStorage`. No personal data is sent to external databases or remote servers.
- **Pilot Controls**: Players can instantly clear and delete all saved profiles and metrics using the "Reset Save" control.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Structure**: Vanilla HTML5.
- **Visuals**: CSS3 Custom Styling featuring glassmorphic overlay widgets, neon palettes, custom typography, scanlines, and CRT screen curvature filters.
- **Game Engine**: HTML5 Canvas running on high-precision `requestAnimationFrame` game loops.
- **Integrations**: Telegram WebApp SDK (`telegram-web-app.js`) and TON Connect UI SDK.
